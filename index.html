Global Summit 2018

The Global Summit is an international conference that aims to cover a wide range of issues related to the latest trends in technology, politics, automation, environment and machine learning. It’s designed for academics at universities, students, entrepreneurs, venture capitalists and anyone who’s interested in enhancing their skills, exchanging perspectives and exploring new ideas. The conference will include topics such as demonstrations, ethical dilemmas of technology, recent advances in specialized areas such as artificial intelligence, nanobots for healthcare and issues of energy conservation. In addition to the invited talks, there will be discussions, idea pitching sessions, and workshops.

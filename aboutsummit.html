Global Summit 2018

The Global Summit is an international conference that aims to cover a wide range of issues related to the latest trends in technology, politics, automation, environment and machine learning. It’s designed for academics at universities, students, entrepreneurs, venture capitalists and anyone who’s interested in enhancing their skills, exchanging perspectives and exploring new ideas. The conference will include topics such as demonstrations, ethical dilemmas of technology, recent advances in specialized areas such as artificial intelligence, nanobots for healthcare and issues of energy conservation.

In addition to the invited talks, there will be discussions, idea pitching sessions, and workshops.

ABOUT

These days, there are so many great ways to learn and sharpen your skills. You can read blogs, listen to podcast, watch Youtube Tutorials and attend webinars, just to name a few. Why then in this day and age should one person bother with the time and expense of an in-person conference or workshop?

If that’s your attitude, then you may be missing out on one of the best opportunities to take your game to the next level. Live events—conferences, workshops, lunch & learns–provide unique learning and career building opportunities that you just can’t find anywhere else.

It enables you to meet experts and influencers face to face, provide ample networking opportunities, new tools, the latest tips and tricks and gives one plenty of opportunity to invest in oneself while having fun.

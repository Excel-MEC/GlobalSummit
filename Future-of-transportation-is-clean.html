Kochi, 2018
The Future of Transportation is Clean
The dawn of driverless cars is upon us. Are you ready to roll?

TRACK DESCRIPTION

Motor vehicles used for transportation are collectively the largest cause of air pollution, responsible for around 50-70 percent of air pollution. Air pollution by itself is a leading cause of a large number of lung diseases, and hurts the environment as a whole, which in turn would lead to even larger problems like climate change, which threatens millions of lives. Thus, it is imperative that we switch to cleaner fuels, failing which might cause irreparable damage to the Earth and countless lives. Hence, at Emerge 2018 we will be exploring the viability of a switch to clean energy and the obstacles that will be faced during this switch. We plan to seek possibilities that could bring about a brighter future for the entire world.

Speaker

Mr. Jesse Manders
Senior R&D Engineer at Nanosys

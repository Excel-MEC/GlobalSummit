Kochi, 2018
The Future of Transportation is Clean
The dawn of driverless cars is upon us. Are you ready to roll?

TRACK DESCRIPTION

Never doubt that a small group of thoughtful, committed people can change the world; indeed, it's the only thing that ever has. We are heading toward a global climate catastrophe unless we change our lifestyle and seriously explore cleaner sources of energy. Aravind Sampathkumaran will explain how there is hope for the transport industry to make this switch sooner than we might expect, at Emerge 2018.

Speaker

Mr. Aravind Sampathkumaran
Head of Software Technolgy, Sun Mobility

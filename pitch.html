Pitch Your Idea

Global Summit 2018 will include an Idea pitching session, where students can pitch innovative ideas to to prominent angel investors and venture capitalists. An elevator pitch is a short description of an idea, product or company that explains the concept in a way such that any listener can understand it in a short period of time. Participants will have 5 minutes to entice a panel of potential investors to invest in their idea, product or company.

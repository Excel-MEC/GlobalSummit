Kochi, 2018
Hatching your ideas: How to build your company
The challenges and success stories of the entrepreneurship world.

TRACK DESCRIPTION

Mark Zuckerberg. Elon Musk. Steve Jobs. Bill Gates. They are still considered to be the Gods of Tech Industry. Ever wondered what sets them apart? They have (had) a concrete plan. For every single day of their lives. It is safe to say that at least one in every million has a revolutionary idea that if realised, would change the face of the world as we know it. Yet, it seldom does happen. Why? The simple truth is that people are scared of the unknown in general. They choose not to try and materialise what's in their mind, because they lack a fool-proof plan. Are you one of them? Do you have an idea that you think would change the face of the world, and yet abandon it because you don't know how to go all the way through? Then get ready to be the next God of the industry. Emerge 2018 presents, "Hatching your ideas: How to grow your company?" a session aimed at helping you develop a concrete plan to take your ideas out of your mind, and lay it out for the whole wide world to see and praise.

Kochi, 2018
The rise of Entrepreneurship
The challenges and success stories of the entrepreneurship world.

Exhibition

Our changing economy has led to the rise of the entrepreneur. Technological innovations make entrepreneurship more accessible to a wider swath of people. The entrepreneur theme is the idea that entrepreneurship involves individuals with unique personality characteristics and abilities. The speaker would be focussing to speak on how entrepreneurship has changed their life, as well as their own entrepreneurial stories. It would also be a discussion about the challenges faced by entrepreneurs.

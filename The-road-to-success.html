Kochi, 2018
The road to success: Follow your dreams
Renewable energy transportation: The need of the hour?

TRACK DESCRIPTION

The busy lifestyle of the tech-savvy era has often found us paying no heed to our dreams. Ever busy with our mechanical life, we try not to meddle with what we truly want, merely concerned with what we need. Have you ever wondered how it would feel to just stop going with the flow and instead, decide to shape your destiny, the way you want? Are you one with an exceptional dream, yet too scared to chase after it? Fret not, for Emerge 2018 brings you "The Road to Success: Follow your dreams", a session for the ones who want to build a life of their own make. This talk is guaranteed to motivate the passion in you, by taking you through the crests and troughs of the life of an entrepreneur who chose to mould his dreams, and make a successful career out of it.

SPEAKER

Bose Krishnamachari
Co-founder of Kochi-Muziris Biennale
Artist. Curator. Entrepreneur. There are several terms that go well with Bose Krishnamachari, internationally acclaimed artist having exhibited in several famed centers such as London Bodhi Art. At Emerge 2018, he hopes to guide us on our road to success, and preach the importance of following our dreams.
"The road to success is always under construction."
-Lily Tomlin

Machine Learning: A new era of Computing
The attractive, intimidate tale of Machine Learning.

TRACK DESCRIPTION

This track is aimed to expand its coverage in the areas of Machine Learning and itâ€™s associated fields. Many companies are turning to machine learning to review vast amounts of data, from evaluating credit for loan applications, to scanning legal contracts for errors, to looking through employee communications with customers to identify bad conduct. The speed at which this is taking place attests to the attractiveness of the technology, but the lack of experience creates real risks. Algorithmic bias is one of the biggest risks because it compromises the very purpose of machine learning. Creating a model that has the same biases as human without the humanity is a recipe for disaster. So for the CS track of the Global Summit we seek to explore the recent advances in the field of machine learning, its applications in the industry, the risk of algorithmic bias and possibility for future developments.
